Murphy Charitable Foundation - Uganda (2024)

The project entailed developing and training on having a fundraising plan. The organization needed a plan to source additional funding capacity to aid in development of their different projects.

Outcome

The organization adopted the funding plan as it allowed them to gain better knowledge on better policy framework approaches. It built their capacity to engage with different stakeholders, enhanced their ability to assess their need for funding and thus guide their growth as an enterprise.
